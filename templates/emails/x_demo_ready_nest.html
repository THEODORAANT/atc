Perch Dashboard

When you log into the Perch admin you log into the Perch Dashboard, rather than the list of site pages found on our other demos. The Perch Dashboard can be enabled in Settings. Most of the apps have a dashboard Widget – for example you can view your forms and download a csv of responses from the dashboard, get quick links through to Blog comments and so on. If your site uses a lot of Apps then the Dashboard can help to give a quick overview.

You can also add additional Widgets to the Dashboard, we have some available for download and you can create your own. They are simple to develop if you have a little PHP knowledge.

Layouts

You may already know Perch, and our method of dropping Perch Regions onto pages as demonstrated in the Swift Demo site. However it is possible to develop sites in a more advanced way using Perch, and a key to this is our Layouts feature. In the Nest demo pages are pretty much HTML free as the common blocks of mark-up of the site are contained in a Layout. You can see these on GitHub, for example the global.above.php layout.

These can then be used to build up a page and save reproducing the same HTML around your Pages. Here is the code for the contact page where you can also see Layout Variables in use.


<?php
include('../perch/runtime.php');

perch_layout('global.above', array(
'title' => perch_page_title(true),
'section_heading' => 'contact',
));

perch_content('Contact form');

perch_layout('global.sidebar', array(
'events' => true,
'members' => true,
));

perch_layout('global.below');
?>

The Members Add-on

Members is one of our newer add-ons for Perch and it lets you create a secure Members area of your site. This can be useful if you want to create a place where people with access can download certain information that you would prefer wasn’t in public view. In the Nest site we have created an area for club members.

Depending on your needs you can create a way for people to request membership, as on this demo, or you could just create members from within the Admin. Registration Forms are templates so you can collect the information that you need. You can also tag members and give access to things on the site by Tag, as explained in the members documentation.

You can even create a secured download area for files that only members should have access to.

The Members App is a free download for anyone with a Perch license – just as with our other apps.
